THE DRIVER

From Oklahoma Dirt to NASCAR ARCA

Michael Maples is a 62-year-old racing veteran from Choctaw, Oklahoma, happily married to Diana Maples. His passion for motorsports began in 1993 with asphalt late model racing in California. After a 21-year hiatus to focus on family, he returned to racing in 2019 with IMCA modifieds.

In 2024, Michael made his dream a reality by entering the ARCA Menards Series with Fast Track Racing. His debut season showed consistent performance and determination, finishing 8th in points with a best finish of 11th.

For 2025, Michael took the ultimate step by forming his own team - Maples Motorsports. As both driver and owner of the #99 Chevrolet, he's building a competitive organization from the ground up, finishing 7th in the championship.

THE TEAM

It all begins with an idea and in 2025 MMS under the leadership of Michael Maples has entered the ARCA Menards Series scene. Michael hails from Oklahoma where he has been racing his Dirt Modified for the past several years. In 2024, Michael took to ARCA for a full season in both the East and Main series to not only learn the series as a driver, but to invest in the future of the series as an upcoming owner. Michael served our country in the Army, and when he is not at the race track he is a entrepreneur in the Oil Industry.

In everything Ryan Roulette does, there are two constant elements: his devotion to service and to his family. Ryan went from being enlisted in the Air Force right out of high school to rising in the ranks as an Officer, and is now the ONLY Air Force officer professionally racing in NASCAR's ARCA series and Craftsman Truck Series. Ryan's devotion to his family and community, the veteran community, are a large part of his life. Giving back to those who served before him, and to those who will carry the torch after his time is done. He has been blessed with the opportunity to perform at the highest levels of racing, and thus veterans giving back to veterans is how he pays it forward. Ryan is thankful for the opportunity to represent MMS this season in the #67 and looks forward to building upon his relationship with Michael.
