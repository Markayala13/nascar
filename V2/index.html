THE DRIVER

From Oklahoma Dirt to NASCAR ARCA

Michael Maples is a 62-year-old racing veteran from Choctaw, Oklahoma. His passion for motorsports began in 1993 with asphalt late model racing in California. After a 21-year hiatus to focus on family, he returned to racing in 2019 with IMCA modifieds.

In 2024, Michael made his dream a reality by entering the ARCA Menards Series with Fast Track Racing. His debut season showed consistent performance and determination, finishing 8th in points with a best finish of 11th.

For 2025, Michael took the ultimate step by forming his own team - Maples Motorsports. As both driver and owner of the #99 Chevrolet, he's building a competitive organization from the ground up, finishing 7th in the championship.
